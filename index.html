About Us

Your Favorite Morning Spot on the Beach

Tucked right along the beautiful Gulf Boulevard in Redington Shores, Kenny's Korner has earned its reputation as the go-to breakfast and lunch spot on the beach. Whether you're fueling up before a morning swim or winding down after a walk on the sand, you'll find fresh, made-with-love food and the friendliest staff around.

From scratch-made hollandaise and house-fresh crab cakes to hearty omelettes and the best French toast on the beach — every dish is crafted with care. Come as you are, flip flops welcome.
