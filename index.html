About Us

A Little Slice of Beach Paradise

Nestled right on beautiful Redington Beach, Kenny's Korner has been the go-to spot for locals and visitors who want fresh, delicious food without the fuss. We're a casual, family-friendly joint where the food is made with love and the views are always on point.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite after a morning on the sand or settling in for a laid-back lunch, you'll feel the warm, welcoming vibe from the moment you walk in. Come as you are — flip flops welcome.
